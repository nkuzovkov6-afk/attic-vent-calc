📋 IRC Section R806
Enclosed attic spaces must have cross-ventilation via openings protected against weather. Net Free Area (NFA) of ventilation must meet the ratio requirements below. Houston follows the IRC with Harris County amendments.
Ratio Requirements
No vapor retarder 1 / 150
Class I/II vapor retarder installed 1 / 300
Balanced 40–60% intake/exhaust 1 / 300 eligible
IRC R806.2: Minimum ventilation = floor area ÷ 150. Reducible to ÷ 300 with vapor retarder + balanced split.

📏 What is NFA?
Net Free Area (NFA) is the actual open area air can pass through — after accounting for screens, louvers, and baffles. Always use the NFA printed on the vent packaging, not the rough opening size. Typical screens reduce open area by 25–50%.
